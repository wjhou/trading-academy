# Trading Cheat Sheets

Quick reference guides for common trading concepts.

## Order Types Cheat Sheet

| Order Type | When to Use | Pros | Cons |
|------------|-------------|------|------|
| **Market** | Need immediate execution | Fast, guaranteed fill | No price control, slippage |
| **Limit** | Want specific price | Price control | May not fill |
| **Stop-Loss** | Protect against losses | Automatic risk management | Can trigger on dips |
| **Stop-Limit** | Price control after trigger | More control | May not fill |

## Candlestick Patterns Cheat Sheet

### Bullish Patterns (Price may go up)

| Pattern | Visual | Meaning |
|---------|--------|---------|
| **Hammer** | Long lower shadow, small body at top | Rejection of lows, buyers stepping in |
| **Bullish Engulfing** | Large green candle engulfs previous red | Strong buying pressure |
| **Morning Star** | 3 candles: down, doji, up | Reversal from downtrend |
| **Three White Soldiers** | 3 consecutive long green candles | Strong uptrend |

### Bearish Patterns (Price may go down)

| Pattern | Visual | Meaning |
|---------|--------|---------|
| **Shooting Star** | Long upper shadow, small body at bottom | Rejection of highs, sellers stepping in |
| **Bearish Engulfing** | Large red candle engulfs previous green | Strong selling pressure |
| **Evening Star** | 3 candles: up, doji, down | Reversal from uptrend |
| **Three Black Crows** | 3 consecutive long red candles | Strong downtrend |

## Technical Indicators Cheat Sheet

| Indicator | Type | Best For | Signals |
|-----------|------|----------|---------|
| **SMA** | Trend | Identifying trend direction | Price above SMA = uptrend |
| **EMA** | Trend | Faster trend detection | More responsive than SMA |
| **RSI** | Momentum | Overbought/oversold | >70 overbought, <30 oversold |
| **MACD** | Momentum | Trend changes | Line crosses signal line |

## Risk Management Cheat Sheet

### Position Sizing Rules

| Account Size | Max Risk Per Trade | Position Size Formula |
|--------------|-------------------|----------------------|
| $10,000 | $100-200 (1-2%) | Risk ÷ (Entry - Stop) |
| $50,000 | $500-1,000 (1-2%) | Risk ÷ (Entry - Stop) |
| $100,000 | $1,000-2,000 (1-2%) | Risk ÷ (Entry - Stop) |

### Stop-Loss Guidelines

| Strategy | Stop-Loss Distance | When to Use |
|----------|-------------------|-------------|
| **Tight** | 1-2% | Day trading, scalping |
| **Medium** | 3-5% | Swing trading |
| **Wide** | 5-10% | Position trading |
| **ATR-based** | 1-2× ATR | Volatility-adjusted |

## Market Hours Cheat Sheet

### US Markets (EST)

| Session | Time | Characteristics |
|---------|------|-----------------|
| **Pre-Market** | 4:00 AM - 9:30 AM | Low volume, wide spreads |
| **Market Open** | 9:30 AM - 10:30 AM | High volatility, most volume |
| **Mid-Day** | 10:30 AM - 3:00 PM | Lower volume, consolidation |
| **Power Hour** | 3:00 PM - 4:00 PM | High volume, trend continuation |
| **After-Hours** | 4:00 PM - 8:00 PM | Low volume, wide spreads |

### Best Times to Trade

- **9:30-11:00 AM**: Highest volume and volatility
- **3:00-4:00 PM**: Second-best time, closing moves
- **Avoid**: First 15 minutes (too volatile for beginners)
- **Avoid**: 12:00-2:00 PM (lunch, low volume)

## Trading Psychology Cheat Sheet

### Emotions to Avoid

| Emotion | Symptom | Solution |
|---------|---------|----------|
| **Fear** | Won't enter trades | Follow your plan, start small |
| **Greed** | Oversized positions | Stick to position sizing rules |
| **FOMO** | Chasing moves | Wait for setups, be patient |
| **Revenge** | Trading after loss | Take a break, review mistakes |
| **Overconfidence** | Ignoring risk | Remember losses happen |

### Trading Rules

1. **Never risk more than 1-2% per trade**
2. **Always use stop-losses**
3. **Let winners run, cut losers short**
4. **Don't trade when emotional**
5. **Keep a trading journal**
6. **Review trades weekly**
7. **Paper trade new strategies first**
8. **Never trade with money you can't afford to lose**

## Quick Formulas

### Risk-Reward Ratio
```
Risk = Entry Price - Stop-Loss
Reward = Take-Profit - Entry Price
Ratio = Reward ÷ Risk

Example:
Entry: $100, Stop: $95, Target: $110
Risk: $5, Reward: $10
Ratio: 2:1 (good!)
```

### Position Size
```
Position Size = Risk Amount ÷ (Entry - Stop-Loss)

Example:
Account: $10,000
Risk: 1% = $100
Entry: $50, Stop: $48
Position Size = $100 ÷ $2 = 50 shares
```

### Profit/Loss Percentage
```
P/L % = ((Exit - Entry) ÷ Entry) × 100

Example:
Entry: $100, Exit: $110
P/L % = ($10 ÷ $100) × 100 = 10%
```

### Sharpe Ratio (Risk-Adjusted Return)
```
Sharpe = (Average Return - Risk-Free Rate) ÷ Standard Deviation

Higher is better (>1 is good, >2 is excellent)
```
